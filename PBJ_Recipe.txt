RECIPE for PEANUT BUTTER and JELLY Sandwich

Take 2 slices of 100% whole wheat bread. Butter each slice on one side only with one teaspoon of peanut butter.
THen add one teaspoon of jelly and spread liberally.
Put two slices together with the buttered side facing inward. Press together until a solid bond is formed.
Et voila! A real peanut butter sandwich.
